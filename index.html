About You Forge It

Things you may see while visiting us

  • Fun is our #1 priority

    Making Sure everyone is enjoying themselves is our number 1 priority. We include everyone possible because smithing is our passion, and would like to pass that on to you.

  • We're Hands on

    Smithing is a hands on fun creative way to make new things.

  • Jewelry

    Build beautiful necklaces, small pieces, and many other intricate crafts.

  • Community

    Enjoy hours of entertainment while crafting and demoing with our professional smiths. Group projects are encouraged.

  • Play with fire

    All of the crafts will be done with authentic smithing anvils and furnaces.

  • Supervision

    While our professional smiths are vigilant in observing safe practices, smithing can be dangerous. It is recommended for young people to have supervision.
